Fastodo

A fast and flexible web-based todo app.
Get Started
No Account Required

Todo

Press Enter to create todo with 4 properties: Name, Date, Priority and Tag. Click circle to mark it as done.

Multiple List

You can create multiple lists for different purposes: daily todo list, long-term goals list or even project features list.

Calendar View

View your todos with calendar. Click on each date to filter the todos on that day.

Sort

You can sort the todos with the properties you just created.

You can access all these features without creating an account!

Your data will be stored on your device locally and not persisted in any database.
